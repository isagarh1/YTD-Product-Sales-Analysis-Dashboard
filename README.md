# YTD-Product-Sales-Analysis-Dashboard Category &amp; Growth Insights :

## Overview

This project presents an end-to-end Power BI dashboard solution designed to analyze product sales performance using Year-to-Date (YTD) metrics. The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of sales trends, category performance, and growth patterns to support data-driven business decisions.

## Objectives
- Analyze YTD sales performance and growth trends
- Identify top-performing categories and products
- Detect underperforming segments
- Understand seasonality and revenue concentration
- Provide actionable business recommendations

## Dashboard Structure
  Page 1: Executive Summary
- YTD Sales with YoY Growth %
- QTD Sales
- YTD Orders & Average Order Value (AOV)
- Monthly Sales Trend
- Category-wise Sales Distribution
- Top 10 Products

Page 2: Deep Dive Analysis
- YTD Growth % by Category
- Category Contribution %
- Scatter Plot (Sales vs Growth vs Reviews)
- Category Performance Table

Page 3: Business Recommendations
- Strategic insights based on analysis
- Growth opportunities and risk areas
- Portfolio diversification suggestions

## Key Insights
- Achieved 33.5% YoY growth, reaching $2.18M in YTD sales
- Revenue is highly concentrated, with Men Shoes contributing ~43%
- Car Accessories identified as a high-growth opportunity
- Categories like Laptop and Mobile & Accessories are declining
- Strong seasonality observed in Q3–Q4

## Business Recommendations
- Expand high-performing categories (e.g., Men Shoes)
- Invest in emerging growth areas (Car Accessories)
- Investigate declining categories for root causes
- Reduce dependency on a single category

## Tools & Technologies
- Power BI (Data Visualization & Dashboarding)
- DAX (Time Intelligence, Growth Calculations)
- Data Modeling & Relationships
